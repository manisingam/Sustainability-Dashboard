SNHU Sustainability Dashboard

Project Overview ▾

Our Sustainability Dashboard brings together SNHU’s STARS data from 2018 through 2022, forecasts future performance for 2023 and 2024, and visualizes key emissions trends. Stakeholders can quickly track progress and plan strategic initiatives.

Goals

  • Aggregate & standardize STARS datasets
  • Visualize historical sustainability points
  • Forecast future performance & emissions

Deliverables

  • Interactive year‑by‑year points chart
  • Emissions trend analysis
  • Predictive dashboards for 2023 & 2024
